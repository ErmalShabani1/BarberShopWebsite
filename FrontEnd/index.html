Services and Fees


HighFade

Price: 20$

Description: A high fade haircut is a stylish and modern haircut that features a gradual tapering of hair length from the top of the head down to the sides and back. The fade starts high on the head, typically around the temples or above, and gradually blends into shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a sharp contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides, resulting in a clean and polished look.

Time Needed Approx: 30 minutes



LowFade

Price: 15$

Description: A low fade haircut is a stylish and modern haircut that features a gradual tapering of hair length from the top of the head down to the sides and back. The fade starts low on the head, typically around the ears or just above them, and gradually blends into shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a subtle contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides, resulting in a clean and polished look.

Time Needed Approx: 25 minutes

ABOUT US

At our barbershop, grooming is more than just a service – it’s an experience. We are a modern barbershop built on passion for style, precision, and respect for every client who walks through our doors. With a team of skilled and experienced barbers, we offer classic cuts, modern fades, beard shaping, and personalized grooming services tailored to every style and preference. Our work is based on quality, cleanliness, and attention to detail that helps you look and feel your best. Our shop provides a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere designed to make you feel at home. Our goal is simple: to bring out your best look and offer an experience that keeps you coming back. Welcome to the place where style meets professionalism!
